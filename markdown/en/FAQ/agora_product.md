Agora is a Platform as a Service (PaaS) provider that enables real-time communicatons with easy-to-embed SDKs and RESTful APIs. Through calling the APIs, you can add the following functions in your applications:

* [Voice Call](https://docs.agora.io/en/Voice/product_voice?platform=All%20Platforms)
* [Video Call](https://docs.agora.io/en/Video/product_video?platform=All%20Platforms)
* [Live Interactive Streaming](https://docs.agora.io/en/Interactive%20Broadcast/product_live?platform=All%20Platforms)
* [Cloud Recording](https://docs.agora.io/en/cloud-recording/product_cloud_recording?platform=Linux)
* [On-premise Recording](https://docs.agora.io/en/Recording/product_recording?platform=Linux)
* [Real-time Messaging](https://docs.agora.io/cn/Real-time-Messaging/product_rtm?platform=All%20Platforms)
